About Us

Meet our company unless miss the opportunity

Established in 1997, Al Harthy Investments LLC (AHI) is a prominent conglomerate in the Sultanate of Oman, recognized for its diversified interests across various industries. The group excels in Media, Marketing Communications, Real Estate, General Trading, Information Technology, and Civil Construction.

AHI is renowned for delivering superior services and optimizing business functions through synergistic operations among its divisions. The company places a strong emphasis on personalized service, integrity in business operations, and values its clients and employees as crucial assets.
